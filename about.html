About Us

The Covidroid, an initiative by Mr Achintya Kumar Satyayan & Mr Akshat Gupta aims to help millions of people worldwide. We started with the same motto of changing the lives of millions of people around the globe.

Our initiative has been very helpful to people in fighting with the COVID-19 Virus. We are providing all our facilities and features free of cost to everyone in need. Our initiative aims to provide people the recent updates regarding coronavirus. We have some articles on health issues on our website which users can have a look and improve their lifestyle. We provide the option of diagnosing to the people who feel they are having some symtomps and we provide them with accurate results.

It's been a wonderful journey altogether. We thought of making a small contribution to the global community in fighting with this virus and that is the thing that drove us in taking such inititaive and making Covidroid. Although , We have a long way to go but if you guys wiil keep us supporting , then definitely our this initiative and further initiatives will touch greater heights.

Thank you for all your love and support.
